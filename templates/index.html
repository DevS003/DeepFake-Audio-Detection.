How It Works

TrueForm analyzes audio using a Convolutional Neural Network trained on the ASVspoof 2019 dataset — 120,000+ samples of real and synthesized speech. The model converts each audio file into a Mel spectrogram, then classifies the acoustic patterns to determine whether a voice is human or AI-generated.
